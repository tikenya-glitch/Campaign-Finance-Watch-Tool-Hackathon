# **Project Name:** CampaignTrust

### Kenya’s Verified Campaign Finance Watch Platform

**Challenge Path:** Challenge 1 – Design & Develop an Innovative Platform

---

# **Executive Summary**

CampaignTrust is a web-based campaign finance verification and monitoring platform designed to enhance transparency, enforce compliance, and strengthen public trust in Kenya’s electoral process.

The platform addresses persistent gaps in the implementation of the Electoral Campaign Finance Act (2013) by introducing a source-authenticated verification system that prevents manipulation of reported figures and enables real-time monitoring of campaign financing activities.

CampaignTrust combines voluntary transparency incentives with automated watchdog oversight — creating a dual-layer system that rewards compliance while exposing irregularities.

---

# **Core Platform Components**

## 1️⃣ Verified Campaign Finance Database

A secure, source-authenticated data system that:

* Integrates with official campaign finance declarations
* Supports read-only submission of financial records
* Prevents manual editing or fabrication of figures
* Updates financial data in near real-time
* Maintains an immutable audit trail for accountability

This creates a trusted database of campaign revenues and expenditures accessible to regulators and citizens.

---

## 2️⃣ Candidate & Party Profiles

Each participating candidate or political party has a structured transparency profile displaying:

* Total funds raised
* Total funds spent
* Categorized expenditure breakdown
* Funding source classification
* Reporting timeline compliance
* Transparency score

Profiles are searchable by constituency, county, and national level, allowing localized public oversight.

---

## 3️⃣ Transparency Leaderboard

A public-facing ranking system that:

* Compares candidates based on disclosure completeness
* Displays spending rates against legal limits
* Tracks funding growth trends over time
* Highlights top transparent campaigns

This reframes transparency as a competitive advantage rather than a compliance burden.

---

## 4️⃣ Compliance Monitoring Engine

An automated system aligned with Article 88(4)(i) of the Constitution that:

* Compares declared spending against legal thresholds
* Flags excess expenditure beyond permitted limits
* Monitors reporting deadlines
* Detects abnormal funding spikes
* Identifies suspicious donation clustering

This component transforms static disclosure into active regulatory intelligence.

---

## 5️⃣ Transparency Badges & Public Incentives

CampaignTrust introduces verification badges:

* 🟢 Verified Filing Submitted
* 🟢 Spending Limit Compliant
* 🟡 Partial Disclosure
* 🔴 No Disclosure

Compliant candidates receive a publicly shareable “Verified Transparent” badge, incentivizing voluntary participation and reputational accountability.

---

## 6️⃣ Watchdog Alerts Portal

A dedicated oversight interface that:

* Publishes flagged anomalies
* Provides downloadable reports for journalists and civil society
* Enables trend analysis across election cycles
* Supports investigative transparency

This strengthens civic monitoring without replacing official institutions.

---

## 7️⃣ Dual-Interface Design

### Public Mode

* Simplified dashboards
* Visualized funding flows
* Candidate comparison tools
* Transparency rankings

### Regulator / Oversight Mode

* Bulk financial analysis
* Risk scoring system
* Compliance alerts
* Reporting gap detection

---

# **Relevance to Legal & Institutional Framework**

CampaignTrust complements:

* The Electoral Campaign Finance Act (2013)
* The Political Parties Act (2011)
* IEBC’s constitutional mandate under Article 88(4)(i)

Rather than duplicating institutional roles, the platform acts as a civic-tech enforcement layer that strengthens implementation capacity.

---

# **Viability & Scalability**

* Modular architecture allows phased national deployment
* Compatible with existing regulatory data structures
* Scalable for 2027 General Elections
* Expandable to include political party funding and public debt transparency

---

# **Innovation**

CampaignTrust introduces a verification-first model to political financing in Kenya by:

* Eliminating unverifiable financial screenshots
* Introducing authenticated, read-only data validation
* Combining incentive-based transparency with automated watchdog oversight
* Transforming compliance reporting into public accountability infrastructure

---

# **Impact**

CampaignTrust shifts political finance from opaque reporting to continuous, verifiable transparency — empowering citizens, supporting regulators, and strengthening Kenya’s democratic integrity.

What is "Misuse of Public Resources"?

Under the Election Offences Act & the Political Parties Act, incumbents cannot use taxpayer-funded assets for private political campaigns.

  • G.K. Vehicles: Using government cars or helicopters to attend political rallies.
  • Public Funds: Diverting CDF or County funds to print campaign materials.
  • Civil Servants: Forcing government employees or police to campaign for a candidate.

Why Your Report Matters

The IEBC relies on citizens to monitor the ground. When you submit a report with visual evidence, UwaziLedger cross-references it against the party's official declared ledger.

If a party holds a mega-rally but declares 0 KES in expenditure, your photo proves they are hiding funds (illicit financing).
